Maggie Stiefvater's November Cakes

Image and recipe found at: click me

The second rendition of Maggie Stiefvater's November Cakes that were featured within her novel the Scorpio Race.

Ingredients

  • <

    For the cake:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/4 + 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 3.5 cups flour
  • 3 teaspoons (or one packet) active dry yeast
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1.5 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs

  • For the filling:

  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon orange extract

  • For the glaze:

  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons whipping cream
  • 8 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of honey

  • For the icing:

  • 1/2 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter

Steps

  1. Microwave milk, water, oil, and butter for two minutes. Add eggs.
  2. In mixing bowl, combine 1.5 cups of flour, the salt, the sugar, and the yeast. Add the liquid and stir. Add last 2 cups of flour one cup at a time and stir between. Knead dough for 8 minutes.
  3. Let dough rise for one hour in a warm place in a greased bowl.
  4. Mix the butter and orange extract for the filling.
  5. Roll out dough to a 12 by 20 inch rectangle. Spoon the filling to cover the rectangle then roll it up. Cut the log into 12 pieces.
  6. Place each roll, cut side up in a greased miffin tin, back in the oven for 30 minutes at 100 degrees fahrenheit.
  7. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and then bake for 14 minutes.
  8. While baking, mix together honey, butter, and sugar in saucepan. Keep on medium heihg heat while stirring constantly. Boil for 2 minutes. Add whipping cream and vanilla, mix well, keep stirring until mixed thoroughly.
  9. Spoon glaze over the rolls.
  10. For the icing, combine all ingredients with enough water to achieve desired consistency in order to drizzle it. Let the caramel honey glaze cool, then with a spoon, drizzle icing in a zig zag pattern. Serve warm.
