Peeta's Stuffed Cheese Buns

Image and recipe found at: click me

A Peeta themed cheesy bread to delight generations. May the odds be ever in your favor!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons yeast
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 8oz of your choice of cheese, recommended to mix it up
  • Parmesan cheese, amount depends on preference
  • A few tablespoons of melted butter mixed with your choice of herbs

Steps

  1. Mix together yeast and water and let it sit for a few minutes.
  2. Add the sugar, garlic powder, melted butter, and oil. Then, slowly add flour a little at a time you mix. Once all the flour is mixed, add salt.
  3. Knead for 10 minutes in the stand mixer.
  4. Set dough aside in a greased bowl covered with a wet cloth for about 20 minutes.
  5. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
  6. Divide the dough into 20 pieces then place about 3/4 inch of cheese in each one. Make sure to pinch the dough around the cheese to tightly seal it up.
  7. Place the pinched side down on a greased baking sheet. Sprinkle buns with more shredded cheese and some parmesan.
  8. Bake for 11-15 minutes untilt he bread is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly. Brush with melted butter and serve warm.
