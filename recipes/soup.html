Sister's Stew

Image and recipe found at: click me

A yummy stew served in bread, themed after Game of Thrones.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 stalks leeks, white and light green parts are 1/2 inch slices
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 large carrot, peeled, 1/2 inch dices
  • 1 large white turnip, 1/2 inch dices
  • 1 large rutabaga, peeled, 1/2 inch dices
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/4 cup barley
  • 3 cups fish stock
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp all purpose flour
  • pinch of saffron
  • 2 cod filets, around 12 ounces total, and cut into bite sized pieces
  • 1 cup roughly chopped clam meat, including any clam juice
  • 2 cups lump crab meat
  • 4 bread bowls

Steps

  1. Add butter and oil in a sauce pan over medium high heat. Then add chopped leeks and cook for 3 minutes before adding garlic, carrots, turnip, and rutabaga. Season with salt and pepper and cook for another 3 minutes.
  2. Add white wine. Add the barley and fish stock. Bring to boil, then bring down to a simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  3. In a small pan, heat the heavy cream with saffron over medium heat. Bring it to a simmer and take off heat. Whisk in the flour and set aside.
  4. Mix cream mixture into pot and stir to combine. Add seafood and then let it simmer for 5-8 minutes. Season once more with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook until desired simmer, if you want it thicker, take out 1/2 cup of soup, and whisk in a tbsp of flour.
